Majoring in biology and wanting to explore into the field of cognitive science as a minor, I have gladly noticed that these subjects are quite heavily invested on at UCL. Besides from the absolutely exciting advanced biology courses like Developmental Neurobiology, The Principle of Cellular Control, and Energy and Evolution, which are highly compatible with specific McGill courses’ syllabus, UCL also provides diverse entre courses for my intended minor of cognitive science: Language and Cognition, Neural Basis of Perception, Memory and Decision, Brain and Behavior... I am aware of the difficulty to fit all these amazing courses into the schedule of only one term, but I will surely strive to gain as much as possible from the lessons that I will take.
